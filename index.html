High Quality Process

Souper Car is a platform designed for the repair and maintenance of cars. On this platform. Site visitors can :

○ Find a professional car repair or maintenance by sector of activity (mechanic, electrician, sheet metal worker, tire specialist...)

○ Find a professional car repair or maintenance by governorate and delegation (Grand Tunis, Ben Arous,Ariana…)

On this platform the customer is a visitor and then becomes a customer when he creates his account. He can:

○ Modify the date of an appointment

Professional is a visitor and becomes a professional when he creates his account as a car repair or maintenance professional. He can :

○ Consult the list of appointments

○ Confirm or cancel an appointment

Administrator can manage the list of users (customers and professionals)
